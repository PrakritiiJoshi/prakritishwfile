Why are cafes the best place to study?

Coffee allows you to stay caffinated and energized to do your work.

Great background noise. Whether it's people talking or chill music, the noise in cafes allows you to drain out your random thoughts and focus on your work.

FREE WiFi-Who can live without WiFi?

Food. You need constant energy to do work and having food you can get near you is extremely beneficial!

You can talk to people. Unlike the library you can talk to people and it's a great place to meet for group projects!
